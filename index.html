Endangered Species

species that are on the verge of extinction due to their this decreasing population on account of environmental or predatory factors are referred to as endangered

House Sparrow

Birds our chirping feathered friends who have amazed us by their aerodynamics and exotic colours are under great threat there are 9934 species of birds that are existing in the world today out of which staggering 1300 (13%) are threatened under extension House Sparrow is one of them

Sparrows, thought tiny are very sensitive and strongly immune birds and their sudden disappearance as sentence or as ecological indicators is a warning to humans about impending environmental hazards

Reasons for the dislocation of sparrows

Non availability of tiny insects as food due to the loss of vegetation

The excessive use of mosquito repellents

Concrete architectures with no nesting sites for sparrows

Air conditioning which leaves no entry or exit points for feeding Sparrow nestlings
